Crypto Development Services FAQ area

Educational content to help you learn about CDS

What wallets can I use to store Crypto Development Services?

There are various wallets (a) simple browser plugins, these work with most browsers, (b) mobile wallets, such as Metamask or TrustWallet. People need to think of a wallet as less of a physical place but more of a special digital address that holds your Crypto Development Servicess. Your public key is your wallet’s receive address and the private key allows the wallet holder to send Crypto Development Servicess.

Can I mine Crypto Development Services?

No, Since CDS is a BEP-20 token issued on the Binance Smart Chain, Polygon & Avalanche networks.

How fast is Crypto Development Services when using it to purchase goods and services?

Every Crypto Development Services transaction can confirm within 5 seconds orless of transmittal, provided the app, wallet, or exchange is connected to the network. Confirmation times are dependent on network congestion.

Are there fees when sending Crypto Development Services from exchanges to wallets and vice versa?

Just the BSC network fee, There are no deflationary fees added to the Crypto Development Services Smart Contract.

Where can I join others interested in talking about Crypto Development Services?

Active community chatter can be found on Discord & Telegram.

How do I contact the team behind the coin about them?

Join Discord, tag an active member who can relay the information to a dev or community member who can help address the issue. There’s no formal governance structure; we are all volunteers but we as a community welcome feedback so that Crypto Development Services can continue to improve and hopefully become a premier cryptocurrency.

Is there a bridge so I can swap my CDS to a different chain?

Yes, We have a bridge setup so you can swap your CDS to another chain. Multichain.org

Is there any farms for CDS?

Yes, We have several farms set up for CDS on yieldfields.finance and ampleswap.com, These are on the Binance Smart Chain Network.
